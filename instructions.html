How to play:

1.Press the 'Roll Dice' button.

2.Take it in turns to roll the dice. Your counter will be move forward the number of spaces shown on the dice.

3.If your counter lands at the bottom of a ladder, you can move up to the top of the ladder.

4.If your counter lands on the head of a snake, you must slide down to the bottom of the snake.

5.The first player to get to the 100 is the winner.

Have fun!
